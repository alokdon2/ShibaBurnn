About the Shiba Burn Tribe

Deep in the forest of Aokigahara there was a “Inugami” [Dog God] like tribe discovered by ケンザ , キング・ラズ and マカベ. This mysterious indigenous tribe who have remained isolated to the present day, maintaining their traditional lifestyle and seems to have a very bizzare spiritual practice. This tribe has a odd way to serve their supreme God 呂氏, precious metal coin burning is part of their sacrificial ritual. They say when the flame of Divine wisdom ignites the soul, the 柴犬 becomes complete. Legend has it that they decended from the God 呂氏 who dwells on planet Sirius and they endeavouring to find a way back home. Sirius, also known as the Dog Star or Sirius A, is the brightest star in Earth’s night sky. The name means “glowing” in Greek — a fitting description, as only a few planets, the full moon and the International Space Station outshine this star. Because Sirius is so bright, it was well-known to the ancients.
